Child soldiers: Children may be recruited or forced to join armed groups and participate in hostilities, either as combatants or in support roles such as cooks, porters, or spies.

Human shields: Children may be used as human shields to protect military installations or personnel from attack.

Propaganda: Children may be used in propaganda campaigns to glorify the actions of an armed group or to demoralize the enemy.

Sexual exploitation: Children may be used for sexual purposes by combatants, either willingly or through coercion.

Forced labor: Children may be forced to work for armed groups, often in dangerous or demeaning conditions.

Detainees: Children may be detained by military or security forces for their alleged involvement in armed conflicts, or for being perceived as a threat to national security.
